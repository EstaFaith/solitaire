							Pyramid Solitaire Rules
1) Cards are deleted in pairs if the sum of their values are 13.

2) The value of the ace - 1, the jack - 11, the queen - 12, the king - 13.

3) Kings are deleted alone.

4)To delete a pair of cards, write their names (KS) one by one through Enter.

5) To change stock - choose 2 (Stock rotation)
   Left card - is the current available card
